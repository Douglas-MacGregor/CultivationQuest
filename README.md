4 realms

- mortal (9 levels)
- soulfire (9 levels)
- starcore (9 levels)
- nascent soul (9 levels)

Each realm has 9 levels, with level 1 being the lowest and level 9 being the highest.
Advancement through levels requires cultivation points, which can be earned through various activities such as meditation, training, and completing quests.

### Realms and Levels

| Realm        | Levels | Description                                                                                    |
| ------------ | ------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Mortal       | 1 - 9  | The initial realm of cultivation, focusing on physical and mental development. stronger mortal |
| Soulfire     | 1 - 9  | A realm that emphasizes harnessing the power of fire and energy. using qi                      |
| Starcore     | 1 - 9  | A realm centered around mastering core energies and inner strength. Internal qi production     |
| Nascent Soul | 1 - 9  | The highest realm, focusing on spiritual enlightenment and soul cultivation. Imortal           |
